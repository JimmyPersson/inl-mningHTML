Company history


Our deep admiration for cars started 1995 when our founder saw his first brand new Ferrari Testarossa F512M roll out of a dealership. The sound of the flat V12 engine that roared with every slight movement of the gaspedal
- a symphony unlike any other.

10 years later we opened our doors with the first of it's kind classic reconditioning workshop for classic cars and a car showroom that would put a grin on any car enthusiasts face.

Our talented employees all have the outmost respect and a burning passion for exclusive and classic cars - we treat your car as the priceless gem that it really is.

John Feelgood - CEO/Founder
